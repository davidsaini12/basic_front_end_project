About This Place

KHURJA

Khurja known as Ceramic City is famous for its ceramics with its surface embellishment in the form of historical and contemporary designs.

Bowl and Mug

Ceramic mugs are manufactured using porcelain, earthenware or stoneware. The material is diluted using water until the clay becomes liquid. The clay is cast into the plaster molds. Filling a mold with clay takes under one minute..


pots

eramic pots are made from fine textured clay and glazed to give a smooth and rich finish..

planter

Ceramic planters are made from clay shaped, glazed, then fired in a kiln. With a light-colored clay, ceramic planters can be produced with bright bold colored glazes, each one unique. Different colored glazes can be blended together to create beautiful flowing patterns.
